## Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer the following operational questions:

1. How has demand changed over time, and does it affect operational performance?
   
   Why it matters:

   Helps Operations anticipate workload spikes.
   Determines whether delays are driven by capacity constraints.

   Potential KPIs:

   Monthly orders
   Order growth rate
   Delivery delay rate by month
   Average fulfillment time by month
   
2. Which stages of the order fulfillment process contribute most to delivery delays?

   Why it matters:

   Directly identifies operational bottlenecks.
   Provides actionable recommendations.

   Potential KPIs:

   Processing time
   Seller fulfillment time
   Shipping time
   Total delivery time
   On-time delivery rate

3. How do delivery delays impact customer satisfaction?

   Why it matters:

   Connects operational performance to customer outcomes.
   Justifies investment in operational improvements.

   Potential KPIs:

   Review score
   Delay days
   Percentage of low-rated orders
   Customer satisfaction by delivery status

## KPI definitions
   1. Total Orders = Number of unique orders.
   2. Revenue = Total sales value.
   3. Average delivery time = Average number of days between purchase and delivery.
