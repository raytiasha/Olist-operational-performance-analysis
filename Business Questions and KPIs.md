## Business Questions and KPI Framework

This analysis aims to answer the following operational questions:

1. How has demand changed over time, and does it affect operational performance?
   
   **Why it matters:**
   1. Helps Operations anticipate workload spikes.
   2. Determines whether delays are driven by capacity constraints.

| KPI                       | Definition                                                       |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Total Orders              | Number of orders placed                                          |
| Monthly Order Growth Rate | Percentage change in orders month-over-month                     |
| Average Fulfillment Time  | Time between purchase and shipment                               |
| Average Delivery Time     | Time between shipment and delivery                               |
| Delayed Delivery Rate     | Percentage of orders delivered after the estimated delivery date |
   
2. Which stages of the order fulfillment process contribute most to delivery delays?

   **Why it matters:**
   1. Directly identifies operational bottlenecks.
   2. Provides actionable recommendations, and enables more efficient allocation of resources.

| KPI                       | Definition                                                     |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Seller Processing Time    | Time from purchase to carrier handoff                          |
| Shipping Time             | Time from carrier pickup to delivery                           |
| Total Delivery Cycle Time | Time from purchase to delivery                                 |
| On-Time Delivery Rate     | Percentage of orders delivered on or before the estimated date |

3. How do delivery delays impact customer satisfaction?

   **Why it matters:**
   1. Helps quantify the customer impact of operational inefficiencies.
   2. Justifies investment in process improvements.

| KPI                             | Definition                                             |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Average Review Score            | Mean customer review rating                            |
| Review Score by Delivery Status | Average rating for on-time vs delayed orders           |
| Low-Rating Percentage           | Percentage of 1- and 2-star reviews                    |
| Delay Duration vs Review Score  | Relationship between delay length and customer ratings |


These three questions were selected because they examine the end-to-end relationship between demand, operational execution, and customer outcomes. Together, they help determine whether increasing order volumes contribute to operational inefficiencies, identify where fulfillment bottlenecks occur, and evaluate the impact of delivery performance on customer satisfaction. Answering these questions enables the Operations Manager to prioritize operational improvements that enhance efficiency and customer experience.
